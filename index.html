Welcome to Among Friends!

Among Friends is an online service that lets you share and receive streaming recommendations with your friends and family.

Instructions

In this prototype you can try the following:

  1. Check out the inbox of received recommendations.
  2. Launch a stream from a recommendation.
  3. Search for streaming media.
  4. Select an item to send recommendations.
